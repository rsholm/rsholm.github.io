# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** June 19, 2026

Daily Calendar ("the App," "we," "our," or "us") is a calendar display app designed to show a user's daily schedule in a dashboard-style view.

This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses, how it is used, and what choices users have.

## Information the App Accesses

### Google Calendar Data

If a user connects their Google account, the App requests read-only access to Google Calendar using this Google OAuth scope:

```text
https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar.readonly
```

This allows the App to read calendar event information such as event titles, event start and end times, all-day event status, and calendar event timing needed to display the daily schedule.

The App does **not** create, edit, delete, or modify calendar events.

### Device Calendar Data

On Android devices, the App may request calendar permission in order to read calendar events available through the device's calendar provider.

### App Settings

The App may store basic local preferences on the device, such as dark mode/light mode and zoom/full-day view selections. These settings are stored locally on the user's device.

## How Information Is Used

Calendar information is used only to display the user's daily calendar inside the App.

The App uses calendar data to show the user's schedule for the current day, display event times and titles, refresh the daily schedule periodically, and provide a kiosk-style calendar dashboard view.

## Data Sharing

Daily Calendar does **not** sell, rent, or share user calendar data with advertisers, data brokers, or third parties.

Calendar data is used only inside the App for the purpose of displaying the user's schedule.

## Data Storage

Daily Calendar does not operate a backend server for storing users' calendar data.

Calendar information is retrieved for display in the App. Basic app preferences may be stored locally on the user's device.

## Google User Data

Daily Calendar's use of Google user data is limited to providing the App's calendar display functionality.

The App's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the [Google API Services User Data Policy](https://developers.google.com/terms/api-services-user-data-policy), including the Limited Use requirements.

Daily Calendar does not use Google user data for advertising, does not sell Google user data, and does not use Google user data to train AI or machine learning models.

## Permissions

The App may request calendar access, internet access, and account-related access where required by Android or Google services to authorize calendar access.

Users may revoke permissions through Android system settings or their Google Account permissions page.

## User Choices and Controls

Users can decline calendar access, revoke calendar permission in Android settings, revoke Google account access from their Google Account permissions page, or uninstall the App to remove locally stored app settings from the device.

## Children’s Privacy

Daily Calendar is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Security

We use reasonable measures to protect information accessed by the App. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

Users should protect access to their device and Google account.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above.
